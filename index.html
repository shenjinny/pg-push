    "Another Great Welcome Reception Starts"

    ANOTHER GREAT WELCOME RECEPTION
    THURSDAY 16TH APRIL 2013
    ONSITE AT THE HOTEL

    With yesterday's amazing Top Achiever Lunch on my mind, I wasn't sure what could possibly be in store for us. But as I followed the scent of an Italian feast wafting through the museum like lobby of the Cavalieri hotel, I knew instantly we were in for another wonderful experience.

    The area was filled with smiles and laughter, the site of good friends and colleagues congratulating ourselves on another year well done! The reception looked amazing, spread through-out the marble foyer, into various rooms and spilling out into the poolside lounge. The lounge like setting outside provided a great opportunity to relax while catching up with old friends I haven't seen since the Bahamas (Winners Circle 2009). While the famous artwork and statues provided a nice back-ground to the many joyous conversations taking place on the inside.

    As the Welcome Reception came to an end, we continued the party at the Circle Club. Great music and good friends - not a bad way to end the night.

    I'm off to the Kick-off breakfast, where Kevin McCloud will be sharing his knowledge on the history and architecture of Rome - this will certainly provide great insight for me, and the Tours and Activities I have planned shortly after.
